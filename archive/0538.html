52th Meeting --MONEY

2014-03-20 第 52 次会议 例会 Meeting

MONEY

Time: Friday Mar. 21th 19:30-21:30
Venue: Room A212, New Main Building, Beihang University


Toastmaster Peter 
General Evaluator Deron
 

There`s a saying goes like: A golden key can open any door. Of course, it`s just a figure of speech, but money is really helpful. If you have enough money, you are more likely to be able to pursue for the things you truly want. If you look into human history, you`ll find that a lot of the greatest physicians and mathematicians were born in rich families, which had ensured that they could focus on their interests and didn`t have to worry about the material condition. All in all, if you can spend your money properly, it will help you a lot in your life. This time, we`ll share our stories and opinions about money.


 Table Topics


Table Topic Master Monk
Table Topic Evaluator Amanda

Prepared Speech   one speaker                                                                                                                            

What you want

CC3 Veronica
Evaluator Keven
 

It is not our abilities that show what we truely are, it is our choices.
                                                                                         —— Professor Albus Dumbledore

In our lives, there are moments when we have to make a choice. Which university should I choose? Which major should I apply for? Should I persue for master study? Should I find a job or keep striving for the PHD degree? If you have no idea what you truly want, you`ll definately be confused with these problems. In this meeting, Veronica will share her opinions on this topic.
