Autumn @BeihangTMC 78th Meeting on Fri Nov 15th

2014-11-13 第 78 次会议 例会 Meeting Autumn

Autumn

Time: 19:30-21:30, Friday, Nov. 15th

Venue: Room A209, New Main Building, Beihang University

Toastmaster: Peter
Table Topic Master: Dale


APEC is over, so is the APEC blue. The fresh air and blue sky was like an illusion: facinating, but somehow unreal. Don`t be nervous, we still have something realistic these days: the central heating! Oh yeah!

Wait! The heating? Is it winter now? Where is my autumn? Where is my autumn with breeze caressing my face and colorful leaves covering all the paths?

Oh dear, it`s gone, as every leave droped from the tree, leaving us with only memory about those beautiful days. In this meeting, Dale will lead us to share our stories about the past autumns.

Prepared Speech

Just Talk About Me

CC1 Jane

Evaluator Sophie

Toastmasters clubs are always characterized by charming ladies, so is our club. This time, we`ll have Jane, an elegant new member, to deliver her first prepared speech. Girls are always mysterious, holding all the secrets in their hearts. But this time, Jane will talk about herself!

Oh my God! Guys, forget about your 11.11, come and get to know a girl!


Lecture

No, Big Deal!

Peter


In the last meeting, Deron shared his experience on how to be a successful Table Topic Master. This time, Peter will continue that serie of experience sharing, and show us his opinions on how to be nice function roles, namely the Timer, the Ah-Counter and the Grammarian, in TMC meetings. If you are a guest or new member of Toastmasters Club, you may wonder: Function roles? They are simple!

"Peter, it`s no big deal, I think."

"No, big deal."
