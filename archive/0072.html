Regular Meeting Time Change Notice

2018-05-09 通知 Notice


      According to constructive advice from dear guests, official team of Beihang TMC decide to change the   time of regular meeting to 19:00-21:00  from this Friday (May 11th) on.  

   During the last several years, our meeting was held from  19:30 to 21:30 every Friday. However, the arrangement of time is no longer suitable for the current situation. We do hope the alteration of time table would be more convenient for you. Contact with us for any question.


Beihang TMC
