Role on the team @Beihang TMC162nd Meeting@19:30 Dec.9 in B221

2016-12-08 第 162 次会议 例会 Meeting Role on the team

Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, December 9th

Venue: Room B221, New Main Building, Beihang University.

Table topic 

Role on the team

Toastmaster: John

Table Topics Master: Bowen

        We always stress that learning to work with a team is very important, especially in modern society with so much competition. But when I am in a team, I always find it is hard to work in a team, maybe you have the same sensation as me. Why? The reality is not consistent with theory! In fact, the strength of a team is big but the premise is that the team is a good team!

        So what is a good team? I think a good team means that everyone in the team clearly know the role he/she takes in the team! Everyone should know his or her ability and everyone is suitable for the role in the team. Sometimes we may doubt whether the role we take in the team is useful or not. But we should know that a good thing need so many people to put heart on it and each role is important! All of us should take responsibility because the team is for all of us, not for a single one. We shouldn’t wait for others to prepare everything, we should participate in preparing everything, which will makes the team better and better! 

        So know your role in the team, and put your heart and soul into the role, then you will feel the strength of your team!

Prepared speech part  (CC)

Milton CC1——Self-introduction

        Milton, a handsome and kind PHD in computer science, will give us a self-introduction! We all want to know who he is and what he is thinking about! In my mind, he is a little shy but he loves Toastermaster very much(This is very important!). All members in our club want to know more about him and someone even want to find a boyfriend like him!         

        We are curious about his experience and strongly expect his self-introduction! 

Kiki CC9——I am busy

      As we grow up, we seem to have less time to do anything we like because we are so busy. . We have to study hard and we have to work hard or we will be abdicated. Being busy is a normal condition now and it is a very good reason for us to refuse to do anything. But when we look back, we always ask ourselves: What we are busy with? We don’t have time to contact with good friends and we don’t have time to relax ourselves, so for what we are busy ? Maybe we should spend some time thinking about how to balance busy condition and happy condition in life.
