2018-05-10 第 196 次会议 例会 Meeting Freedom

Meeting Role Play


Toastmaster : Samuel 


Table Topic Master: Tanner

 Table topic

Theme: Freedom 

What is freedom? We are born to become free. Freedom is defined from different aspects, and freedom varies from one culture to another. Some people define freedom as a natural right, with which human beings are born. Everyone wants to be free and independent from others. Freedom is the right to do what one wants, to live where he wants, to eat what he wants, to learn what he wants, and to choose the religion in which he believes, without ignoring or harming other rights.


Please share with us your story and opinions on Friday, 11th May
