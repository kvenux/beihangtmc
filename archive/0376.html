New Member Introduction-- Kiki

2015-09-17 其他随笔



Attention please!!! Here comes a mysterious lady, one who is so professional about Karate & Kiwi, a well-known boy killer, our new member——Kiki!


“I am Kiki from University of Science and Technology Beijing. I am a senior student and major in Environmental Engineering.


I heard about Toastmasters when I was in Guangzhou in September 2014. Influenced by the high passion of all members there, I had joined the Sysugz Toastmasters for half a year. Then I returned back to Beijing. One day, I found the Beihang Toastmasters by accident on the internet. After attending a meeting as a guest,I decided to continue my toastmasters journey from here. Cause I felt the same kind, friendly and active, though the two journeys start in different cities and there are different people.


It is my honor to be a new member of the big family and I believe I can learn a lot from all of you guys.”
