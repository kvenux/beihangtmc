Emotion@Beihang TMC 190th Metting

2018-03-15 例会 Meeting Emotion


Time: 20:00-21:45, Friday, 16th March
Venue: Room A218, New Main Building, Beihang University

____________________________________________


Table Topic 

Theme: Emotion

Table Topics Master: Samuel

Emotion is a strong feeling such as love, fear or anger. Positive emotions can keep us in good state and improve work efficiency, but negative ones may drag us down. All of these make up our colorful life.So, let’s pay attention to what emotion we are in and discuss it.

____________________________________________


Prepared speech


Speaker: Star 

Theme:    CC3. A hard beginning


The first step is always difficult，especially after a so enjoyable winter holiday for us students.  So how to overcome the laziness and reluctance to plunge into the new semester, as well as greeting the forthcoming bright spring ?
