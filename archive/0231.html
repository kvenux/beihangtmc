Chat Software@BeihangTMC 143rd Meeting 19:30 July 1st G849

2016-07-01 第 143 次会议 例会 Meeting Chat Software
Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, July 1st 
Venue: Room G849, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Chat Software
Toastmaster: Cicily
Table Topic Master: Amy
         Millions years ago, we travelled from place to place to talk to each other; later, we had wired telephone attached to the wall so that we can talk to others without seeing their faces; later on, we could even contact with our friends simply by texting. And now, we have QQ, WeChat, Weibo, Facebook and all kinds of chat software.
     On the one hand, we benefit from its convenience, however, there still are some shortcomings.On the good side, we can spread the information of those people in need of help through WeChat or Weibo so that they can get enough money or materials they need as fast as possible. On the other side, some people would use the great influence of those software to spread rumors or unhealthy information to get money, which is pretty damageable to our society.
    So, what should be the right attitude for us to face those chat software? What is the right way for us to use them to the greatest benefit? We sincerely welcome you to join us in this meeting and look forward to hear your opinion about this issue.
Prepared speech part  (CC)
Sarah CC5——
A special interview to the Prime Minister of UK
       In this meeting, Sarah is going to share with us her special interview to the Prime Minister of UK. Did she really have an interview with Cameron? How did she get the chance to meet him? What questions had she asked? I bet you are as curious as I am right now. Let’s listen to her speech together and find the answers.
Francis CC2——
I forgot what happiness feels like
      Few years ago, CCTV held an interview, in which it asked people whether or not they feel happiness in their life. Are you happy? What is your definition of happiness? Sometimes, we can’t tell if we are happy or even forget what happiness feels like. Francis also has this problem. Let’s listen to his confusion and opinion about it.
Cicily CC3——
My experience of trading with Huang Niu
I guess everybody has some experience of buying concert tickets on Damai or from Huang Niu. Have you been successful every time you trade with them? Tonight, let’s hear from Cicily, a huge fan for Bigbang, about her experience of trading with Huang Niu.
