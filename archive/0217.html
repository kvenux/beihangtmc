Job@BeihangTMC 148th Meeting 19:30 August 12th G849

2016-08-11 第 148 次会议 例会 Meeting Job

Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, August 12th


Venue: Room G849, New Main Building, Beihang University.


Table Topic Section
Theme：Job
Toastmaster：Amy
Table Topic Master：John
    During the summer vacation, some of my friends choose to go for an internship with the expectations that it can help them improve abilities to deal with some matters and be familiar to the job they would like to do. It can been seen that a job has an important influence for our life. For the life’s significance lies not in the possession of money, it lies in the joy of the work,the thrill of our job,the joy and moral stimulation of the work will never been neglected.
    So what kind of job would you like or what is your impressive stories in your job?For the theme of job,you must have a lot to talk with us.Come and share with your inner opinions, we are waiting for you this Friday!

Prepared Speech Section
行走是一种态度！
   Vance 【Change myself】
   Every one wants to be a better man,in the countless night,we got self-examination,and in the daytime,have you ever done some actions to get the new goal? Today our gentleman-Vance will tell us his understanding about the change,looking forward to his story of how he changed himself!

   Huan【You are stronger than you think.】
  After a low profile in the last semester,our goddess-Huan will begin her journey of the prepared speech. In her ice break speech,she will tell us a true creed-”you are stronger than you think”.Yes! Every one has the “”prehistoric powers“”,let’s see how does Huan find her mystic energy!
