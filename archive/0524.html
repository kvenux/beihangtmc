Contest & Competition

Time: Friday May.30 19:30-21:30

Venue: Room A212, New Main Building, Beihang University


Toastmaster: Peter
Table Topic Master: Veronica


We are desendants of winners. For millions of years, our ancestors had been struggling to defeat their opponents for the single reason of surviving and reproducing. Nowadays, we are still fascinated with the idea of contest, or competition. Not only for material benefits, but also for honor. This time, we`ll talk about the contests and competitions we face in our lives.


Prepared Speeches 2 speakers

Meditations

CC2: Dale
Evaluator: Keven

About two thousand years ago, there was a great king of Roman Empire called Marcus Aurelius. He was a king honored for his deligency and battle achievements. However, he is now only famous for his masterpiece called Meditations. All your achievements may vanish, but your thoughts can last forever. Let`s wait for dale`s story!

Emergency Tips

CC1: Jack
Evaluator: Peter

Life is not always going smoothly, there are accients all around. When you are in an accident, the foremost thing for you is to have an idea of how to save the others. In his CC1, Jack will give us some tips on it. Hopfully, his speech will help us in the future.
