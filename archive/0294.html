Sheldon Like@BeihangTMC 130th Meeting 19:30 Mar.25

2016-03-24 第 130 次会议 例会 Meeting Sheldon Like


Time: 19:30-21:30,Friday,March,25
Venue: Room B208, New Main Building, Beihang University


Sheldon Like


Toastmaster Deron

Whether you have even seen the Big Bang and Sheldon, there's no doubt that you have friends of Sheldon like, who's IQ > 10EQ. They are smart, kind-hearted, frank, but know nothing about how to deal with females. They might be programmers, engineers, or any kind of bookworms, dressed samely and unimpressive. Tonight we are goona look into their deep minds, to find out another face. Are you curious? Come to join us at 7:30pm in Beihang Toastermasters! 

By the way, it's free!


Table Topic Master Vance

Have you ever tried table topic speech? Steping onto the stage and answering a question randomly in front of 30 more listeners is a thing deserve to try. You can even select a male or female partner to deal with it together! Brilliant Vance has prepared several amusing questions for you. You see, you come, can you conquer it?


Competent Communication Projects (CC)


My Life Long Career (CC-1)

Speaker: Qiqi

It's one of the happiest thing to devote your life in a career that you enjoy. It's also a hard case for graduates to choose a career. This time our new member Qiqi decides to share her attitudes. Can you guess what her life long career is? Come and get the answer!


Will Virtual Reality Be Popular in the Future? (CC-2)

Speaker: Sarah

Most of the engineers will be crazy if he got a laboratory like iron man! While one of the supportive technologies in it, which has also been a hot topic recently, is virtual reality (VR). From a female programmer's view, what is essential for VR and will it be next trand? Friday night, Our pretty Sarah will show you something new!



My Emotional Doctor (CC-3)

Speaker: Gastby

As a sheldon like shy lad, Gastby has something kept in heart deeply. Who is his emotional doctor? A goddess of wisdom or an adorable elfin? Time to findout his small secret! Prying into other's inner heart is always attractive after all. You won't regret for coming this time!


What is Toastmasters?

Toastmasters International (TI) is a nonprofit educational organization that operates clubs worldwide for thepurpose of helping members improve their communication, public speaking, andleadership skills.
