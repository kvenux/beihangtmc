2016-09-08 第 152 次会议 例会 Meeting Competition
Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, September 9th 
Venue: Room B208, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Competition
Toastmaster: Sarah
Table Topics Master: Joy
    Last week, we just lanched a memerable competition and won fruitful results. Five members got promoted which broke the record of Beihang Toastmaster history.
    Speaking of competition, have you ever attended a contest? How do you feel about it? Would you like to share your experience with us? Do you want to promote your skills? Come to Beihang Toastermaster Club! Our beautiful table topic master Joy will be waiting for you. 
Prepared speech part  (CC)
Brittney  CC1—— I am Brittney
    Here comes another ice breaker! Brittney is a new member of our club. She's going to show herself and get herself remembered. Would you like to know our beautiful Brittney more? Come to Beihang Toastermaster Club!
Amy (CC3)——
Cease to Be Free and You Cease to Live
Liberty, love! These two I need.
For my love I will sacrifice life,

For liberty I will sacrifice my love.

Do you still remember the famous poem? From the poem, we can see the author’s high evaluation for freedom, and so do Amy. 
    On Friday night, Amy is gonna share her story with us. Are you longing for freedom? Come to Beihang Toastermaster Club!
What is Toastmasters?
