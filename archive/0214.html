Lotalty@BeihangTMC 149th Meeting 19:30 August 19th G849

2016-08-18 第 149 次会议 例会 Meeting Lotalty
Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, August 19th 
Venue: Room G849, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Loyalty
Toastmaster: Peter
Table Topics Master: Grace
    The talk of loyalty has long existed throughout human history and is having the Chinese social media buzzing because of an actor’s divorce from his wife recently.
    You would have to admit the fact that different people coming from different cultural background have different understanding toward love, friendship, partnership and etc., therefore, different thought on loyalty in these relationships.
    Some say loyalty is gold, the only thing that should be tested by the everlasting time; some consider loyalty shackles, the very one thing that would imprison the unyielding freedom.
    Now, this is not about right or wrong, good or bad, it is just different. Come and tell what’s on your mind. 
Prepared speech part  (CC)
Speaker1: Mike
    We keep learning in every stage of life through every possible means. Mike was learning a lot from his experiences of sports. This Friday, he will be sharing two of his ‘sporty’ stories and lessons he has learnt with you.
Speaker2:Andy
    In Chinese we have 缘分（yuan’fen） that brings us together. Have you ever wondered how and why someone should walk into or out of your life? Andy has, and he will be sharing with us the coming Friday.
