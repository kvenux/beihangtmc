Choice@Beihang TMC 153th Meeting@19:30 September 23

2016-09-22 第 153 次会议 例会 Meeting Choice

Time: 19:30-21:30,Friday,September,23
Venue:Room B221,New Main Building,Beihang University


Choice



Choice

TM:Winston

TTM:John


Life is like a long journey. We have many choices to make at the crossroads, the choice of further study or finding a job, love or career, righteousness or benefit, innovation or conservatism, offering or obtaining, right or wrong, love or death … We choose different roads so we have different life. Do you have some unforgettable memories about making a choice? Come to our meeting this Friday evening and share your story with us!



Speech 1
Huan CC2


My pets


Raising pets is a good way to reduce our loneliness. The lovely animals can be our good companions. Our member Huan have raised many pets since she was a child. What was the fate of Huan’s pets? What interesting things have happened between Huan and her pets? Let’s listento Huan’s CC2 speech.

Speech 2
Jocelyn CC3


Judge a book by its cover?



When choosing a book, will you judge it just by its cover without reading its content? When meeting a person for the first time, will you judge him or her merely by his or her appearance without learning more about him or her? Is this a society that appearance matters most? This Friday our intellectual member Jocelyn will share her opinion about this topic. Let’s look forward to Jocelyn’s CC3 speech.

Speech 3
Andy CC6


 To Be or Not to Be




“To be or not to be”, these six words are the most famous in all the literature of the world. They are also the most famous words in Shakespeare because Hamlet was speaking not only for himself but also for every thinking man and woman. To be or not to be, that is the question. This Friday Andy will answer this classical question in his CC6 speech. Come here and get the answer!


What is Toastmasters?
Toastmasters International (TI) is a non-profit educational organization that operates clubs worldwide for thepurpose of helping members improve their communication, public speaking, and leadership skills.
