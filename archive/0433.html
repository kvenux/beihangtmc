Toastmasters Club各职位介绍

2015-06-11 其他随笔



Officers Intro.

All officers are elected for either six months or one year, at the Club’s decision.Terms of office are from January 1st to June 30th and July 1st to December 31st for clubs with six-month terms, or July 1st to June 30th for clubs with annual officer terms.




PRESIDENT

The President presides at meetings of the Club, has general supervision of the operations of the Club. Serves as one of the Club’s representatives on Area and District Councils.

大老板就不用多介绍了。劳心劳力，从上到下，事无巨细都得过问和参与；当然啦，锻炼和成就也是大大的。想要当主席的同学一定要人见人爱花见花开的精力无穷多。




VPE

VPE---Vice President Education

Second ranking officer of Club. Plans and directs club programs which meet the educational needs of the Club members. Plans and publishes regular schedules of meeting assignments. Keeps track of member's progress towards goals. Serves as one of the Club’s representatives on Area and District Councils.

专门负责每次会议的备稿演讲者安排和俱乐部每个人成长进度的监督和记录哦！由于他/她的工作，每个人才可以真的按部就班的完成演讲，获得进步！




VPM

VPM---Vice President Membership

Third ranking officer. Plans and directs programs to retain and increase club membership. Serves as one of the Club’s representatives on Area and District Councils.

负责每次会议接待和入会的就是他们啦！话说他们还掌握着全俱乐部每个人的各种个人信息，你懂的。。。


VPPR

VPPR---Vice President Public Relations

Fourth ranking officer. Develops and directs programs that inform individual members and the general public about Toastmasters International and about Club activities.

俱乐部的喉舌和大嗓门，专门负责俱乐部的各种消息发布，信息通知，OUTING等等。。。




Secretary

The Secretary is responsible for Club records and correspondence. Maintains the club roster. Has custody of the Club’s charter,Constitution, Bylaws, and all other records and documents of the club. Keeps an accurate record of the meetings and activities of the Club.

俱乐部的会议纪要和物品保管都是他/她啦！很辛苦的工作，但是获得的锻炼也是不容小视的，一个团队需要无私默默奉献的队员。也许他/她不那么闪光，但是绝对缺一不可！




Treasurer

The Treasurer is responsible for Club financial policies, procedures and controls. Collects dues and pays dues to Toastmasters International, and maintains records. Makes financial reports to the Club at least quarterly. Receives and disburses, with approval of the Club, all Club funds.

会费大大的交给他们哦！不过他们可是很公正和有原则的人哦。。。俱乐部花销一分一毛记录的清清楚楚。


SAA

Prepares meeting room for meeting. Maintains Club property, including banner, nametags, and supplies. Make agendas for every meeting.Greets visitors. Chairs Social and Reception Committees.

每次最辛苦他们为俱乐部会议和活动提前布置场地,准备会议进程表，欢迎新客人和维持秩序！

Beihang Toastmasters Club

是帮助每一位会员提升“领导力”与“沟通能力”，给每位成员鼓励、支持和温暖，见证你点滴成长的地方！！！

会议流程：

1. PREPARED SPEECH (备稿演讲，仅会员可参加）。

2. TABLE TOPICS（即兴演讲，所有与会者均可参加）。

3. EVALUATION(评价环节，会对每个参加演讲的人进行点评和提升的专业建议）

活动时间：每周五晚7：30-9:30（请留意微信平台具体信息）

活动地点：北京市海淀区学院路37号，北京航空航天大学新主楼B208（地铁十号线西土城或知春路站下）
