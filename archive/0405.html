One-child@Beihang TMC 105th Meeting@19:30 July 24

2015-07-24 第 105 次会议 例会 Meeting One-child

Time: 19:30-21:30,Friday,July 24

Venue: Room G849,New Main Building,Beihang University

One ch


In 20th century, China began to carry out the one-child policy as a measure to control the fast population growth. And it used to be effective and beneficial under certain social condition. But with the passage of time, an increasing number of problems caused by the one-child policy begins to emerge, such as the sex-based birth rate disparity, adoption, “Four-Two-One”problem and other potential social problems. As most of us belong to one-child families,what is your personal experience? Come on and share with us!

Speech 1
Bowen CC3


 Far Away from Home

Where is your home? How long is it since you began to leave away home? In future, where will you live? With our growth, most of us began to leave home and live in different cities because of study, work or even the girlfriend. Far away from home, we sometimes miss our home and our families. At the same time, we also think about these questions: where will we go? Shall we return home or go to other cities? What’s Bowen’s feeling about this ? Let’s welcome Bowen’s sharing.

Speech 2
Jill CC5


Free Yourself from Worries

Worrying is like a rocking chair；it gives you something to do, but it gets you nowhere. --Glenn Turner. Worries can take over your life if you allow it to.Whether we like it or not, constant stress and worry can physically make us sick. What’s Jill’s opinions about the relation between ourselves and the worry?Why do we have to avoid too many worries? Let us listen to Jill’s speech!
