New Member-- Rita Kuang

2015-09-16 其他随笔

I am Kiki from University of Science and Technology Beijing. I am a senior student and major in Environmental Engineering. I heard about Toastmasters when I was in Guangzhou in September 2014. Influenced by the high passion of all members there, I had joined the Sysugztoastmasters for half a year. Then I returned back to Beijing. One day, I found the Beihang Toastmasters by accident on the internet. After attending a meeting as a guest,I decided to continue my toastmasters journey from here. Cause I felt the same kind、friendly and active, though the two journeys start in different cities and there are different people. It is my honor to be a new member of the big family and I believe I can learn a lot from all of you guys.
