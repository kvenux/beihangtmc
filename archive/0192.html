R.M.B.@Beihang TMC 156th Meeting@19:30 Oct. 21 in A208

2016-10-20 第 156 次会议 例会 Meeting R.M.B.


Time: 19:30-21:30, Friday, 21 Oct.
Venue: Room A208, New Main Building, Beihang University


Do you want to know why there are no pictrures in this letter? Answer lies in the end...


R.M.B.

Toastmaster - Deron

When the book stores are full of startups and making money guides, while your pocket is still slender, it's time to rethink. This time we decide to have fun with this infamous and love-hate shit. Rather than boring discussions on a small goal of making your first 100 million, you might be asked to spend some. Curious? You'll regret for hesitating now.


TABLE TOPIC

Table Topic Master - Jocelyn

"What about always want to spend more than I earned?" When asked if there were any unpleasant things recently, Jocelyn gave me the answer. That's how the theme was born. Instead of a trite Table Topic, Jocelyn decided to bring something new. If you miss her show, what's the meaning of keeping alive?


COMPETENT COMMUNICATION

She (CC 4)

Speaker - Britney

How it feels like to keep delivering speech for 4 weeks? Ask Britney. Last week this girl forced her to make a change. Here she comes. Britney said she would neither discuss friendship, nor bromance. What's left? Answer would be here waiting for you...


Attitude Matters (CC 9)

Speaker - Sarah

Even through no more information is given, I can still tell this will be a motivation speech. As a female technician, Sarah has some unique view aspects of life. This time she might be able to prick your idleness and pessimism. If you need energy, the oil here is free!


Humor (CC 9)

Speaker - Kiki

Does this title suggest that Kiki prefers a humor boyfriend? Or it just means Kiki thought she is a humor girl, which actually is not a bad joke. I should admit this speech does make me curious. What will a girl with humor disability tell us? I can't wait to know it!
