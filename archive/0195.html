Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, Octorber 14th 
Venue: Room  A208, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Self-image
Toastmaster: Peter
Table Topics Master: Joshua
    Maxwell Maltz wrote in his book that self-image is ‘your key to living a better life’ (Maltz, 2008). What are you like in the eyes of yours? Meanwhile, the fact that we live in a society rather than isolated lands allows others to also have an image about you. Both images can be either barrier or motivator. 
    Toastmasters will gather to share how they keep their self-images on track this Friday (14th) night at 19:30 @ A208, New Main Building, Beihang University.
