2015-07-09 第 103 次会议 例会 Meeting Summer

Time: 19:30-21:30, Friday, July 10
Venue: Room B208, New Main Building, Beihang University


Summer


Toastmaster: Elena
Table Topic Master: Junyar


Summer

Summer is supposed to be colorful: colorful plants, colorful dresses, and colorful stories. Summer is vibrant also: watermelons being ripe, birds flying around, and cicadas singing in the tree. While summer is fickle sometimes: furious thunder and lightning, suddenly pouring rains and burning suntans. What appears on your mind when it comes to summer? Sunshine, beach, sexy girls, or ice-creams? Looking forward to your sharing with us!


Speech 1
Emily CC2


Simplify Your Life

Imagining one day, all things that bother you, such as television programmers, Internet, games, E-mails, messages, phone calls, and so on, disappear in your life, what will you do? I think I'd find a quiet afternoon, take a cup of tea and read a novel waiting for me for a long time. Simplified life makes us feel peaceful and happy. Come and see the way in which Emily simplifies her life.


Speech 2
Nancy CC3


Step Over Fear

Fear is a room, only when out of which, can we see the sun. Sometimes we fear because of our unknown or imperfection. Fear is not a good thing, which makes us put off working, miss a good opportunity, or indulge ourselves in something. If we could go through fear, what will happen? Let's listen to Nancy.
