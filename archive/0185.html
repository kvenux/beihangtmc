Winter@Beihang TMC158th Meeting@19:30 Nov.4 in B208

2016-11-03 第 158 次会议 例会 Meeting Winter
Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, November 4th 
Venue: Room  B208, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Winter
Toastmaster: John
Table Topics Master: Amy
 If winter comes, can spring be far behind? Winter is coming quietly and unconsciously in Beijing, and we have to put on our coats and gloves. Winter is a beautiful season because when it is snowing, the whole world becomes white and it is so fascinating! But winter also makes us annoyed for it is so cold, which makes us unwilling to go outside! So do you like winter or not? How do you like winter? this is an interesting topic for us to discuss!
Prepared Speech (CC)
Speaker 1: Gatsby CC4－－ Can you? 
Do you still believe that you are superman and you can achieve anything without effort? Do you still imagine you are good at everything even though reality shows that you are not? Growing up means that you know your actual strength rather than overestimate yourself. It is very important for everyone to know what you can achieve and what you cannot achieve. Don’t pretend to be something if you cannot.
Speaker 2: Joshua－－Day Dreamers 
We have a lot of imaginary and we want to get everything we like. But the fact is that we always imagine a lot without doing anything. Day dreamers are such people that they think too much, but do nothing. So are you a day dreamer or do you have the same characteristics like day dreamers? 
Here Joshua will give you his views about day dreamers
What is Toastmasters?
Toastmasters International (TI) is a non-profit educational organization that operates clubs worldwide for thepurpose of helping members improve their communication, public speaking, and leadership skills.
