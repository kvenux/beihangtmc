Spring Festival@Beihang TMC 165th Meeting 19:30 Mar.3rd G849

2017-03-03 第 165 次会议 例会 Meeting Spring Festival

Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, March 3rd

Venue: Room  G849, New Main Building, Beihang University.


March 3rd

Spring is coming，We are back.


Table topic 

Spring Festival

Toastmaster: Fiona

Table Topics Master: Flair

    "If winter comes,Can spring be far behind?"As a chinese, Spring Festival is our favourite holiday in a year, because it means that we can go home to get together with our families. We may  like to have a meal with them, talk with parents, grandparents, brothers or sisters, which is the most important  relationships in our life, and enjoy the air of each other's company. Otherwise, Spring Festival means that the old year has passed away and the new year is in the corner.

    During Spring Festival, We poured back home from all  directions,Share with families  a year's ups and downs. So, what did you feel on Spring Festival, happy or sad ? Did you have some deep touching moments to share with us?And what is you new  year's plan?Exercising? Travelling? Making more money or looking for a girl friend?

    So in the begining of 2017, Are you ready? Please come to BeiHang Toastmaster and share with us!

Prepared Speech (CC)

Speaker 1: Milton CC2－－ Ferryman

    Milton will introduce himself from childhood to now when he met a lot of ferryman who led him out of life's dilemma one after another, the ferryman such as his grandparents, parents, mentors, friends, and so on, it is these ferry people to guide him, so that he can become a more sunshine and confident person.

    He also hopes that he can become more excellent, mybe at some point he could become someone else's ferryman.

Speaker 2: Cystal CC5－－ "Terrible" Photos 

    If I showed you a picture of yourself versus a mirror image of yourself and asked which one you'd like more, the answer is very strong. The latter one, right? But, why? There is nothing wrong with the image or mirror. Indeed, the so-called mere-exposure effect should be blame for that.

What is Toastmasters?

Toastmasters International (TI) is a non-profit educational organization that operates clubs worldwide for thepurpose of helping members improve their communication, public speaking, and leadership skills.
