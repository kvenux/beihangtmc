Regular Meeting Time Change Notice

2018-05-07 官员竞选 Election

Your attention please! 


Our regular meeting was held by every Friday from 19:30 -21:30. In order to provide more convenience to our guests, Beihang Toastmaster officer team decide to change our regular meeting time, please be advised that from This Friday May 11(th),our meeting time will changed to 19:00-21:00  the meeting would be held at New Main Building, Beihang University. 




Beihang TMC
