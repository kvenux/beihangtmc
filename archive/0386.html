Trouble Maker
Human beings can be divided into two categories: trouble makers and trouble solvers. Trouble maker is someone getting others or oneself into trouble, just like Wang Bao in the film of Lost in Thailand. Most of the time, we really dislike and try to stay away from trouble makers, just as Xu Lang always trying to do with Wang Bao. In real world, no matter who you are, you must have met trouble makers or you may have been a trouble maker yourself sometimes. Toastmaster(she) is curious about the scenes and stories related to trouble makers in your minds. Welcome to our meeting and enjoy sharing your stories and opinions with us!!!
1
Prepared Speech
Rita CC2
He has a dream
----- Rita
Dream, an amazing word, is also a popular theme especially in our generation. We expect dream to help us change our current situation, achieve our values, and have a better life. We are willing to say “I have a dream, and that is blabla ”. Whlie sometimes listening to others’ dreams can also give us inspirations. Today, Rita will offer us such a chance in her CC2, and this may remind you of your own dream. Let's listen to “He has a dream”.
2
Prepared Speech
Jill CC6
Sth you'd never say
----- Jill
Words are like a double-edged sword. Some of them are kind, but some are hurtful. However, kind and warm words can be misunderstood as scolding ones in certain emotional situation. More often than not, scolding words may come out of our mouth before we regret for them. We are likely to say what we shouldn’t say to those we love. What specific words shouldn’t be said and how to avoid them? Jill is ready to share her experience and suggestions with us.
