【CC手册】CC1-破冰之旅Ice Breaker

2017-04-12 回顾 / 总结

【CC1】Ice Breaker

【实施要点】在第一次演讲中，你将向俱乐部会友介绍自己，提供自己的一些信息，如背景，爱好以及抱负。你可以先在朋友或家人前进行演讲演练，并尽量与一些听众建立目光交流。在正式做演讲时，如果愿意你也可以使用便笺。通读整个单元内容再准备你的演讲。
【演讲目标】

1、开始在观众前进行演讲；

2、发现演讲者已经具备的演讲技巧以及在以后需要关注的技巧；

3、向俱乐部会友介绍自己。

【时长限制】5-7分钟

【Summary】For your first speech project, you will introduce yourself to your fellow club members and give them some information about your background, interests, and ambitions. Practice giving your speech to friends or family members, and strive to make eye contact with some of your audience. You may use notes during your speech if you wish. Read the entire project before preparing your talk. 

【Objectives】

1、To begin speaking before an audience; 

2、To discover speaking skills you already have and skills that need some attention; 

3、To introduce yourself to your fellow club members.

【Time(minutes)】5-7
