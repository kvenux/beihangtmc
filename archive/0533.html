54th meeting -We Media

2014-04-04 第 54 次会议 例会 Meeting

We Media

 Time: Friday April.4 19:30-21:30 

 Venue: Room A212, New Main Building, Beihang University

Toastmaster: Deron
General evaluator: Amanda

We media is a way of understanding how general public provide and share facts and news of their own, after they are strengthened by numerical technology and connected with the global information system. Weblog, wechat, facebook etc. are all patterns of we media. It has deeply affected the world nowadays. This time we are going to discuss about we media. Waiting for you Friday night! 

     

Table Topics

Table Topic Master: Jason
Table Topic Evaluator: Monk          

   

Wen Zhang, Ma Yili and Yao Di's personal issue has been open to the public and shocked the public. In the war between Ma and Yao, we media has been the weapon of them and the public. If you are one of them, how will you use we media power to protect your own benefit? Interesting questions from talented and handsome Jason are waiting for you!        

          

Prepared Speech


CC5 Peter
Never wait until it is too late


Peter is a thoughtful and humorous speaker who can always bring us new and suggestive ideas. This time he wants to tell us why we should never wait until it is too late. Wait for what? No idea yet. The answer lies in his speech this Friday. Same time, same room, new friends, and new surprise!
