Linguistic Charm@BeihangTMC 125nd Meeting @19:30 Jan. 8th

2016-01-07 第 125 次会议 例会 Meeting Linguistic Charm

Time: 19:30-21:30,Friday,Janurary,8
Venue:Room B104,New Main Building,Beihang University

Linguistic Charm

TM：Vance

TTM：Joy


Language is the tool of communication among people. We use language every day. It delivers our emotion, our ideas and ourculture. Different people in different country use different language. But there is a common thing. If you want to communicate better, you should pay attention to your linguistic charm. What is linguistic charm? How to make your words be charming? Come to our meeting this Friday evening. Discuss with us and we will learn more.

Gatsby

Be a rational fan

CC2


Do you know flute? Do you know Shuiyueru?In our club, there is a handsome boy who is good at flute. His name is Gatsby.This Friday, Gatsby will tell us his story of being a fans of Shuiyueru. If you want know more about Shuiyueru and Gatsby, just come to our meeting this Friday.

Bloomfield

Fever

CC2

It’s very common for us to have a cold.Cold is not a big problem. But it can influence our life and work. If we have a cold, we will not pay attention to what we are doing. Also it’s not comfortable, especially when we are having a trip. This Friday Bloomfield will tell us how to address this problem when we are having a cold.
