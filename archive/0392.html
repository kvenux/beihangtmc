Career Planning

TM:Winston

TTM:Jill

Career planning is the ongoing process that you plan your career systematically. It consists of activities and actions that you take to achieve your career goals. Although the future cannot be foreseen, aspecific career planning will surely help you face the uncertainties and difficulties that lies ahead in your career. Have you ever planned your career seriously? What is your ideal career? What is your advantage in this career? How will you strive for it? Come to our meeting this Friday evening and share with us!


Speech 1
Rita CC1


Self-introduction

This Friday evening our new member Rita will give her CC1 speech. Rita is a cute and unique girl. I believe we are all impressed by her special pose when she ends her speech. Do you want to know more about her? How about her family? Does she have any brothers or sisters? Does she have a boyfriend? Let’s listen to Rita’s self-introduction.

Speech 2
Jack CC8


The Island of Smile

Across the Taiwan Strait lies the “treasure land” of our motherland. It is called Taiwan Island. It’s a beautiful island of the Pacific Ocean. People there speak the same language and share the same culture with us. Everything there affects the hearts of people on the mainland. This Friday Jack will share his journey to Taiwan. What place did he visit in Taiwan? Was there anything interesting happened in his journey? Let’s look forward to Jack’s speech.
