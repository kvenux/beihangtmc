Superpower@BeihangTMC 147th Meeting 19:30 July 29th G849

2016-07-29 第 147 次会议 例会 Meeting Superpower
Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, July 29th 
Venue: Room G849, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Superpower
Toastmaster: Joshua
Table Topics Master: Qiqi
    Do you like super hero movies?  Superman, batman, spiderman.....So many super heroes who own huge fantastic super power--Flying, minds reading, invisible, super strength and so on.  We always get attracted by when we watch these movies. Somehow, we like these super heroes because they have all kinds of super powers that we can’t own ourselves. 
    However, nothing can stop our imagination. Have you dreamed of owning some super powers yourself some day? What kind of super power do you want to own most? What will you do ?What will the world become if some of us have super powers? Are you interested in these fancy questions? Do you want to use your imagination and wisdom to persuade or ever conquer all people? Then come to join Toastmaster this Friday!
Prepared speech part  (CC)
Francis CC2—— I’m tired
    Living in modern world, everyone is so busy on all kinds of stuff.  Everyday we have to get up early in the morning, with so much work to do. 
    Francis, a tall and handsome boy, feels tired like everyone these days. This week, he will give his CC2 speech: I’m tired..What makes him tired? And how will he deal with his tiredness? Very interesting topic!So don’t miss his speech this Friday!
Sarah CC7——The man I want to marry
      Along with the growing of my age, lots of friends who cares about me would like to set me blind dates. Before that, we would have a conversation as follow. "what kind of man would you prefer?"Every time I give them the same answer,"Well...I have no idea, and I don't have any requirements.""Oh dear, no requirement often means you are too demanding." 
    Today I began to think about this question seriously to help me have a clear vision because I don't want to end up being alone for the rest of my life.
Qiqi CC5——  English, 7F and me
    We have learned English so many years. Each of us must have his or her own stories with English. These stories can be funny, interesting, or even sad sometimes. 
    This week, Qiqi is going to share her stories with English. What kind of stories ? Are there good tips about learning English? And What does 7F mean exactly? Come and find answers in Qiqi’s speech.
What is Toastmasters?
