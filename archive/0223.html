Aloneness@BeihangTMC 145nd Meeting 19:30 July 15th G849

2016-07-15 第 145 次会议 例会 Meeting Aloneness
Time：19:30-21:30, Friday, July 15th 
Venue: Room G849, New Main Building, Beihang University.
Table topic 
Aloneness
Toastmaster: Joy
Table Topics Master: Emma (from SparkTime TMC)
    Someone says, humans are strange creatures, they are afraid of darkness, so they learned how to control fire; they fear wildness and loneliness, so they build cities, skyscrapers and a complicated modern world. We are not along anymore, we all live in an iron and concrete forest fulfilled with lights and music, man and women, roads and bridges….but why still we can’t get rid of the aloneness in our deep heart?
    Someone says, real aloneness is not one's loneliness, but the loss of oneself in the endless noise. I totally agree with that, it is such a world sometimes I really cannot understand. Maybe being lost is the best way of release.
    Who knows?
    Ladies and gentlemen, following the joint meeting of Sparktime and Beihang TMC last week, tomorrow we will have GC TMC members to join with us. Toastmasters is the perfect harbor I can ever park whenever I need to be recognized and understood, whenever I need encouragement and supports. We know how to conquer loneliness since we embrace each other, we share our own experiences about controlling aloneness, even learn how to benefit from it. Tomorrow we share our own feelings about aloneness and I am very curious about what kind of sparkles would appear among those 3 clubs? Come and join in us tomorrow at G849 Main Building Beihang University, the best is here waiting for you.

Prepared speech part  (CC)
Peter CC1——
Survive the Hopelessness
    As like aloneness, there are some moments that we feel hopelessness in our lives. Nine times out of ten, life is not easy. How can we get through those tough periods, to open the door of hope? Today, a handsome and professional boy, Peter, will share the tips with us.
Cicily CC4——
My passion for English
    From Cicily’s previous speeches, we not only got to know about her better, but also felt her strong passion in English. Are you curious how she felt in love with this language and what she has done to improve her English efficiency? Let’s look forward to her sharing this Friday!
Qiqi CC4——
Tom’s three lessons
    This week, Qiqi will talk about three important lessons that Tom taught to her. Do you want to know who’s Tom? The cat Tom ? Or the famous and handsome actor Tom Cruise. There are so many Toms in the world. Then what lessons did Tom teach to Qiqi? Never fight with the Mouse Jerry, like Tom did in the series? Or disguise yourself to become a good spy, like Tom did in the movies? There are so many questions about the speech. So just come and join us, to find answers in Qiqi’s speech.
What is Toastmasters?
    Toastmasters International (TI) is a nonprofit educational organization that operates clubs worldwide for thepurpose of helping members improve their communication, public speaking, and leadership skills.
