Root of Exhaustion@BeihangTMC 176th Meeting 19:30 Jun.9 A218

2017-06-08 第 176 次会议 例会 Meeting Root of Exhaustion

Time：19:30-21:00, Friday, June 9th 

Venue: Room A218, New Main Building, Beihang University.

Table topic 

The root of exhuastion

Toastmaster: Fiona

Table Topics Master: Milton

    There is no doubt that exhaustion is a pressing concern today, with some particularly startling figures emerging from emotionally draining sectors such as healthcare. It is common  we feel tired during every single hour of the day and that the mere thought of work in the morning left us feeling exhausted. So what is the root of our exhaustion? Tonight Milton will talk about where our exhaustion comes from with us .

Prepared speech part  (CC)

Sarah AC1——Lessons from being a leader

    Ever since we learnt to speak and work together, some members of the group have tried to become ‘leaders’. But what is a leader?In an article in Nigeria’s Premium Times, Bamidele Ademola-Olateju states:

A leader goes in the front, leads the way and by his actions; people follow.                                                                                              

Today Sarah wants to share us about her lessons about being a leader. Let us look forward to it.

Carrie CC7—Live like a kid

    I remember when I was a child and feeling like I couldn’t wait to be an adult because I would get to do what I wanted to, whenever I wanted to do it.  Now I grow up but I don't feel happy like how I was before. LIving like a child sometimes brings us happiness and satisfactions. 

    Tonight Carrie will talk about this topic and look forwad to it.
