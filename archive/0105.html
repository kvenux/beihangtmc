Table topic 

Self-discipline

Toastmaster: Rocky

Table Topics Master: Milton


    

                            


Everybody has to heard this word "self-discipline".Yes, there's no denying that it is neccesary for us. However, how many of you guy can carry out the spirit of self-discipline ? And how do you resist yourself to do something wrong? Milton is going to talk this topic with us and raise some questions to us.
